Utah

Utah is the state that looks like a giant chair in the western United States. In the south you can find a desert type of climate, and more north is much greener and snows in the winter time. The entire state is riddled with mountains as it is located as part of the Rocky Mountains. there are multiple national parks that have amazing sites that you won't find anywhere else.

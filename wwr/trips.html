Our couples trip is perfect for anyone who wants to spend some quality time on the river with your significant other. Take in majestic views on a calming and relaxing journey down to the south end of the bend and back. With unlimited rental time you can take a break and enjoy a nice picnic on any part of the riverbank. A quick training on how to navigate the river is optional before embarking on this unforgettable experience. Don't worry about the gear, it's all included in the rental fee. Just worry about feeling the experience! rental fee: $350 picnic addition: $79

Our adventure rafting trip is for those who love adrenaline and, of course, getting wet! Anyone over the age of 10 is allowed on the trip with an adult acting as responsible guardian from start to finish. The trip takes you through the ups and downs of anything and everything about river rafting with rapids for up to 5 hours. Feel the excitement of being at the mercy of mother nature as you travel down the winding 26.8 mile journey of your lifetime! A river guide will be present with the group at all times, a small sack lunch will be served about 2 hours into the experience. All rafting gear will be provided prior to ebarking on the river waters. package deal: $299.99

Our waterfall scenary trip is for those who want to enjoy a similar experience as our adventure rafting journey, but with a much less bumpy expedition. Enjoy everything nature has to offer as you embark down this majestic voyage through the 12.5 mile passage all the way up to a marvelous view of the cascading Utah falls. You'll have the opportunity to spend about an hour around the Utah falls and pool area to soak in this memorable adventure. Professional photos are offered at a discount price! All rafting gear and training are provided and covered in the rental fee. rental fee: starting at $249.99 professional photogarphy: starting at $99.99
